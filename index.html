MindLock Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority

Overview

MindLock is a productivity tool designed to help you maintain focus and control your digital environment. We're committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information and use your data.

Bottom Line: We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. All your information stays on your device.

What Data Do We Collect?

MindLock stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Blocked Websites: URLs you choose to block during focus sessions
  • Focus Tasks: Personal goals and task lists you create
  • Session Data: Focus session duration, timestamps, and attempt counters
  • Statistics: Streak counts, daily session totals, and weekly hours tracked
  • Master Password: Hashed password for settings protection (not transmitted)
  • User Settings: Deep Work Mode toggle and other preferences

How Do We Store Your Data?

All data is stored exclusively using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API, which ensures:

  • Data never leaves your device
  • Data is not accessible to third parties
  • Data is protected by your device's security
  • Data is encrypted in transit between your browser tabs

Do We Share Your Data?

No. We absolutely do not:

  • Sell or lease your data to third parties
  • Share your blocked sites, tasks, or statistics
  • Transmit any data to external servers
  • Use tracking or analytics tools
  • Collect diagnostic information without permission

Third-Party Services

MindLock does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Your data is never shared with external parties.

Data Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • You can delete any blocked site from your list at any time
  • You can clear all data by uninstalling the extension
  • Uninstalling automatically removes all stored data

Security

MindLock uses industry-standard security practices:

  • Master password protection for settings (hashed, never transmitted)
  • Chrome's built-in storage encryption
  • No external API calls or server communication
  • Regular security updates through Chrome extensions policy

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify users of any significant changes through extension updates. Continued use of MindLock following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new privacy policy.
